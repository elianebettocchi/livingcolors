TUTORIAL
























- OK, IT CAN BE RACISM. I'LL SUBPOENA THEM. BY THE WAY YOU DESCRIBED THEM, THEY COULD BE THE SAME GUYS WHO ASSAULTED THE GIRL.
Three days later you go back to the police station and the chief is already questioning the guys. One is indifferent and the other defiant, as there are no witnesses and they were not recognized by the victim's grandmother. Therefore, they can wait for trial out of custody.
Outside the police station, two guys in a bike shout at you:
- FELLING POWERFUL AINT'YA SHITTY NIGGAS? YOU'LL SEE ON TOMORROW'S NEWS WHAT AWIATS YOUR WITCH OF A COUNCILWOMAN!!!
- YO! WASN'T OUR COUNCILWOMAN SUPPOSED TO GIVE A SPEECH TODAY? DOES THAT MEAN...



- I'M SORRY, I CAN'T FRAME IT AS RACISM. I'M GOING TO SUBPOENA THE GUYS TO RESPOND TO THE ASSAULT PROCESS.
Three days later you go back to the police station and the chief is already questioning the guys. One is confident and the other defiant, as there are no witnesses and they were not recognized by the victim's grandmother. Therefore, they can wait for trial for assault out of custody.
Outside the police station, two guys in a bike shout at you:
- FELLING POWERFUL AINT'YA SHITTY NIGGAS? YOU'LL SEE ON TOMORROW'S NEWS WHAT AWIATS YOUR WITCH OF A COUNCILWOMAN!!!
- YO! WASN'T OUR COUNCILWOMAN SUPPOSED TO GIVE A SPEECH TODAY? DOES THAT MEAN...



- YOU'RE RIGHT, IT'S RACISM. I'LL SUBPOENA THEM. BY THE WAY YOU DESCRIBED THEM, THEY COULD BE THE SAME GUYS WHO ASSAULTED THE GIRL.
Three days later you go back to the police station and the chief is already questioning the guys. If the result was 16 open shells, the attacked girl's grandmother recognizes them and they are arrested.
Outside the police station, two guys in a bike shout at you:
- FELLING POWERFUL AINT'YA SHITTY NIGGAS? YOU'LL SEE ON TOMORROW'S NEWS WHAT AWIATS YOUR WITCH OF A COUNCILWOMAN!!!
- YO! WASN'T OUR COUNCILWOMAN SUPPOSED TO GIVE A SPEECH TODAY? DOES THAT MEAN...
