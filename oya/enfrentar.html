TUTORIAL
























A police siren brings back those who were in a trance and you see people around accusing the guys of aggression. If the result was 16 open shells, the police catch up and arrest the guys in the act. You run back to the temple.
- DISGUSTING! I SUGGEST THAT YOU ASK MOTHER IRIDESSA, WHO IS PART OF OUR COUNCILWOMAN'S COMITEE, TO ACCOMPANY YOU TO THE POLICE STATION.
You agree and an hour later you are at the police station giving your statement. Mother Iridessa is already there.
- YOU'RE RIGHT, I'LL FILE A BOLO OF AGGRESSION MOTIVATED BY RACISM AND RELIGIOUS INTOLERANCE.
Outside the police station, two guys in a bike shout at you:
- FELLING POWERFUL AINT'YA SHITTY NIGGAS? YOU'LL SEE ON TOMORROW'S NEWS WHAT AWIATS YOUR WITCH OF A COUNCILWOMAN!!!
- YO! WASN'T OUR COUNCILWOMAN SUPPOSED TO GIVE A SPEECH TODAY? DOES THAT MEAN...



You and the guys run away before the police arrive. Three days later, you receive a subpoena to give evidence about an assault scene.
You show up at the police station and Ya-Kekere Iridessa T'Oya, who works at the community's councilwoman committee, is already there to help you.
- THERE'S THIS COMPLAINT FILED AGAINST YOU SO I NEED YOUR VERSIONS. BUT REST REASSURED THAT SEVERAL WITNESSES HAVE ALREADY TESTIFIED IN YOUR FAVOR CLAIMING SELF-DEFENSE. THE CASE WILL BE CLOSED.
Outside the police station, two guys in a bike shout at you:
- FELLING POWERFUL AINT'YA SHITTY NIGGAS? YOU'LL SEE ON TOMORROW'S NEWS WHAT AWIATS YOUR WITCH OF A COUNCILWOMAN!!!
- YO! WASN'T OUR COUNCILWOMAN SUPPOSED TO GIVE A SPEECH TODAY? DOES THAT MEAN...
