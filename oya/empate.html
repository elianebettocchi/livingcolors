- BEFORE THE GUY COULD FIRE, I KICKED ONE OF THE SCAFFOLDING RAILS, SENDING A PILE OF JUNK FALLING, CREATING A CLOUD OF LIME DUST. WE HEARD A SHOT, WE RAN INTO A BAR, THE MOTORCYCLE STARTED, AND A CROWD SCREAMED AND RAN AND SOON THEY OVERPOWERED THE GUY, UNTIL THE POLICE ARRIVED. UNFORTUNATELY, MOTHER IRIDESSA WAS GRAZED AND IS RECOVERING IN HOSPITAL.
Draw again: 8 or more, Iridessa warned the counselor, who canceled the event. Less than 8, the counselor was not warned and suffered an attack but survived.
Father John Ti Shango makes a speech about the importance of activism and unity among the candomble devotees and pays homage to Mother Iridessa. Everyone applauds with concern and sends a lot of ashe to her, you and Thiago [whomever spent points of magic power recovers everything].
The uncertainty about the relationship between the three criminal acts still hangs in the air: the stoning on the girl, the attempted murder on Iridessa and the possible attack on the councilwoman. Is it worth investigating?
Experience Points for each character:
- 2 Management points to increase skills;
- 1 point of Ethics to rise idealism. If the councilor suffered the attack, 1 negative point of Ethics to increase radicalism.
