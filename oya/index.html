Iridessa, the Ya Kekere (second mother) of the terreiro, asks Thiago and you to go out and buy the last items left for the party.
Almost at the shop, on the other side of the street, a group [the same number of players] of white boys wearing designer clothes shout at you:
- JUJU MAKERS!



- ARE YOU GONNA MAKE JUJU FOR SOMEONE? YA'LL GOING TO HELL!!!
- LET IT GO. IT AIN'T WORTH IT TO GET PISSED WITH THESE PEOPLE.
- THAT'S WHAT YOU GET FROM NIGGA'S RELIGION, BRUH!



- RACISM AND RELIGIOUS INTOLERANCE IN THE SAME CURSE?
- RACIST? WHO ARE YOU CALLING A RACIST, ASSHOLE? I HAVE BLACK FRIENDS AND EVEN DATED A BLACK CHICK! WE SHOULD SUE YA FOR LIBEL!
- NOW WHAT? EITHER WE LEAVE IT OR WE GO RIGHT NOW TO THE POLICE STATION REPORT THESE GUYS FOR RACISM AND RELIGIOUS INTOLERANCE?
- IF YOU GO TO THE POLICE STATION WE'LL BEAT THE SHIT OUT OF YOU!
